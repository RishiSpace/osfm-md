Privacy Policy for OSFM Markdown Editor

At OSFM Markdown Editor, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the data you create and store using our cloud-based, client-side encrypted service. We want you to understand our practices and feel secure while using our application.

1. Information We Handle

When you use OSFM Markdown Editor, the primary information we handle is the markdown content you create and choose to save to the cloud. It's important to understand that:

  • **Your Markdown Content:** This includes the text, formatting, and any other data you input into the editor.
  • **Encrypted Data:** Before your content is transmitted to our cloud storage, it is encrypted directly within your web browser using encryption keys that are under your control. We do not have access to your unencrypted content or your encryption keys.

We do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email address, or IP address unless explicitly stated in a separate section of this policy (e.g., for account creation or communication, if applicable). This policy focuses on the handling of your encrypted content.

2. How We Use Your Encrypted Information

The encrypted markdown content you save is primarily used for:

  • **Cloud Storage and Synchronization:** To store your encrypted data securely in the cloud and allow you to access it from different devices.
  • **Facilitating Collaboration (if applicable):** If the editor offers collaboration features, your encrypted content may be shared with other users you explicitly authorize, with decryption still occurring client-side.

**We do not access, decrypt, or analyze the content of your markdown files stored in the cloud.** The encryption process ensures that your data remains private and inaccessible to us.

3. Data Security and Encryption

We take significant measures to protect the security of your *encrypted* data in transit and at rest on our servers. These measures include industry-standard security protocols and practices. However, it's crucial to understand that the security of your *unencrypted* data and encryption keys rests with you.

Key aspects of our security approach include:

  • **Client-Side Encryption:** Your content is encrypted in your browser *before* it is sent to our servers, ensuring that we only ever handle encrypted data.
  • **Secure Transmission:** We use secure protocols (e.g., HTTPS) for all data transmission between your browser and our servers.
  • **Secure Storage:** We employ robust security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the encrypted data stored on our cloud infrastructure.

**Important:** We strongly advise you to use strong, unique encryption keys and to store them securely. If you lose your encryption keys, your data may become irretrievable.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your encrypted data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will take steps to remove your encrypted data from our systems. However, please note that there might be residual copies of data in our backup systems for a limited time for security and disaster recovery purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

We may utilize third-party services for infrastructure, data storage, or other functionalities necessary to provide OSFM Markdown Editor. We carefully select these providers and strive to ensure they maintain high standards of security and privacy. However, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

6. Your Control Over Your Data

You have significant control over your encrypted data:

  • **Creation and Editing:** You can create, edit, and manage your markdown content within the editor.
  • **Saving and Deleting:** You can save your encrypted content to the cloud and delete it when you choose.
  • **Account Management (if applicable):** You may have options to manage your account and the data associated with it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any revisions on this page, and the updated policy will be effective upon posting. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
